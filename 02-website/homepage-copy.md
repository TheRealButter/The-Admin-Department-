## Problem section

## Your admin is probably scattered across too many places.

WhatsApp. Gmail. Spreadsheets. Notebooks. Staff memory. Phone calls. Old folders. Random PDFs.

That is how things get missed.

- Leads are not followed up.
- Invoices sit in email.
- Client onboarding steps are forgotten.
- Tenant or customer messages get buried.
- Documents are missing when needed.
- Owners ask for updates and nobody has a clean answer.

The problem is not always your people. Most teams simply do not have a proper admin system.

## How it works

1. **Audit your admin** — we review the current process and identify where work gets lost or repeated.
2. **Map the workflow** — we define what should happen, who owns it, and what needs tracking.
3. **Build the system** — we set up the tracker, forms, templates, automations, approval flows, and reports.
4. **Train your team** — we show your team exactly how to use it.
5. **Support monthly** — we monitor, improve, and adjust the system as your admin changes.
