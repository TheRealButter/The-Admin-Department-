# Offer — Invoice Admin Setup

## Product name

Invoice Admin Setup

## One-line promise

Stop losing invoices in email. Get supplier documents captured, checked, filed, and ready for approval.

## Who this is for

- bookkeepers
- accountants
- property managers
- construction companies
- admin-heavy SMEs
- tender/admin-heavy businesses
- small retailers and wholesalers

## What problem it fixes

Supplier invoices, receipts, proof of payments, and statements arrive in different places. Some are in Gmail, some are downloaded by staff, some are sent on WhatsApp, and some sit in folders without clear status.

The result:

- invoices get missed
- documents are filed inconsistently
- approval is delayed
- missing information is not chased
- duplicates slip through
- owners do not know what needs attention

## What we install

- invoice tracker
- supplier register
- exception queue
- approval workflow
- folder structure
- document naming rules
- missing-info templates
- approval request templates
- weekly report template
- simple handover SOP

- We do not replace your accountant or bookkeeper.
- We do not execute payments.

- We do not guarantee tax/VAT correctness.
