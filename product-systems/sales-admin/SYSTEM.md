# Sales Admin System

## Product promise

Stop losing enquiries because replies, follow-ups, and quotes are scattered across WhatsApp, email, calls, and spreadsheets.

## Best-fit clients

- contractors
- plumbers
- electricians
- gate/garage/security installers
- cleaning companies
- repair businesses
- small agencies
- consultants
- real estate and rental teams

## Pain this system fixes

Businesses receive enquiries from multiple places, but nobody has a clean view of who replied, who needs a quote, who needs follow-up, and which opportunities are going cold.

## Workflow

1. New enquiry is captured from form, phone note, WhatsApp, email, DM, or manual entry.
2. Contact and enquiry details are added to the tracker.
3. Lead is assigned a source, urgency, service type, owner, and status.
4. First response is prepared or logged.
5. Quote/request stage is tracked.
6. Follow-up due dates are created.
7. Leads are moved through statuses until won, lost, cold, or closed.
8. Weekly report shows leads received, contacted, quoted, won, lost, and overdue follow-ups.

## Statuses

- New
- Contacted
- Needs Response
- Waiting for Client
- Quote Needed
- Quote Sent
- Follow-up Due
- Won
- Lost
- Cold
- Closed

## Human approval rule

The system may prepare replies and follow-up messages, but a person must approve/send messages until the workflow is proven.

## MVP install components

- enquiry tracker
- contact tracker
- quote/follow-up queue
- message templates
- weekly sales admin report
- SOP
- demo data

## Automation candidates after manual validation

- website form intake
- Gmail lead intake
- lead urgency scoring
- WhatsApp click-to-send links
- follow-up reminders
- missed-response alerts
- weekly pipeline report

## What not to promise

- This does not guarantee sales.
- This is not a full CRM replacement at MVP stage.
- This does not send autonomous messages without approval.

## Success metrics

- enquiries captured
- average first response time
- leads contacted
- quotes sent
- overdue follow-ups reduced
- won/lost outcomes tracked
- estimated lost leads prevented
